Challenge 5: Accessibility Reflection

Challenge 3 could have accessibility problems because of the various types of user inputs required. It would be difficult for someone who is unable to type responses to fill out the first form offered. Additionally, it would be difficult for someone who has issues with a steady hand or with hand movements to select check boxes or bullets. Finally, anyone who is visually impaired likely would need larger text offered or an audio option in order to fill out the form.
